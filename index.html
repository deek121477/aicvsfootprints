ABOUT US

What is AICVS?

AICVS is the Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision Society of Cummins College of Engineering. We at AICVS adopt an enthusiastic and dedicated approach to learn and put in the effort, as we start from the very basics, and develop our skills as we move forward. We at AICVS conduct various practical workshops, guest lectures, seminars, Kaggle competitions, that all the students can participate in.

Mission

To introduce students to the thriving domain of AI and Computer Vision. We aim to equip them with primary skillset in Deep Learning and various algorithms. We aim to guide to learn to implement these techniques and better understand what happens behind the scenes.

How AI ML has changed the world

From the research we do on the Internet to the goods that come to our door with a single click, to recommendations on Netflix, AI technology is directly in our lives. AI is applied in all sectors including healthcare, banking, logistics and travel industry to provide superior experience. The latest developments in Artificial Intelligence are chatbots, smart cars and IoT devices. AI has gone from a science-fiction dream to a critical part of our everyday lives.
